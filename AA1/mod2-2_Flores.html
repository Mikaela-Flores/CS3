A Silent Voice

The Silent Voice is the story of Shoya, the tormentor, and Shouko, a person with an impaired hearing. She is bullied mercilessly, she transfers to another school. Years later, one of her former tormentors, Shoya, sets out to make amends. Along the way, Shoya meets new people that either help or stops him from reconciling with Shouko.
