Badminton

  • Badminton is a sport that uses a racket and a shuttlecock
  • It is played with a rally or hitting the target back and forth until it drops to the ground
  • There are two rounds per game and in each round, one of the players needs to have a maximum score of 21 and the other player to have 19 for the game to proceed to the next half
